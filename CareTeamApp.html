    PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

    Care Team Connect Mobile App


    OVERVIEW

    There are over 5.8 million Americans living with Alzheimerâ€™s Disease or a related dementia; these are long-term diseases that require an average of 4-8 years of care. Recent trends show that a vast majority of people diagnosed with dementia prefer to receive support in their home. As such, there is a growing demand for in-home dementia care services.


    SOLUTION

    The team at Generation Connect partnered with one of the leading providers of non-medical, private duty home care to develop a mobile application to support the personalization of dementia care services.





    OUTCOMES
    • Conducted initial interviews and developed prototype within 4 weeks.
    • Used prototype to acquire $150,000 of investment and pilot group of home care agencies.
    • Launched native apps for iOS and Android in less than six months.
    • Pilot group data indicated the tablet engagement improved client mood outcomes, reduced caregiver turnover, and extended client length of stay in home.
    • Pilot data was published in research study in partnership with the University of Wisconsin.
